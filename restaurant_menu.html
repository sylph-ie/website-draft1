Menu

Peri Chicken

Try our grilled tender chicken marinated in our special blend of herbs! A certified fan-favourite for many of our regulars! Like all our main dishes, it can be served on its own (solo meal) or as a combo (with a side dish and soft drink/water).

Prices:
Solo meal: $6.00 | Combo meal (+ side dish and soft drink/water): $9.00

Tandoori Chicken

For a more savoury alternative to the Peri Chicken, try our roasted Tandoori Chicken marinated in a yogurt and spice mixture, cooked inside our in-house tandoor-like oven! Comes with mint sauce on the side and its sizzle plate served with caramalised onions. It's sizzling plate is a must-have for a crowd-pleasing dish to add to your table! (We personally recommend ordering it with garlic-mayo sauce (plot twist: there's no mayo in it!) and a side dish of rice...).

Prices:
Solo meal: $6.00 | Combo meal (+ side dish and soft drink/water): $9.50

Grilled Lamb

For all lamb-lovers out there, try out our smokey and tender, grilled lamb! Served in a sizzling hot plate with caramelised onions. Want a tester? Get 500 g of this! A full-course? 1 kg!

Prices:
500 g: Solo meal: $12.00 | Combo meal (+ side dish and soft drink/water): $15.00
1 kg: Solo meal: $21.00 | Combo meal (+ side dish and soft drink/water): $25.00
